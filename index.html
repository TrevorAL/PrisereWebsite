Introducing Prisere: Your Comprehensive Disaster Resilience and Insurance Claims App

Prepare your business for the unexpected with Prisere - the ultimate app designed to empower small and medium-sized businesses (SMBs) like yours in times of disaster. Our powerful features ensure a seamless business interruption claim process, offering financial protection and peace of mind when you need it most.

Required Features for Smooth Business Interruption Claims:

  • Business Interruption Claim Management: Easily handle revenue and expense data with seamless integration from QuickBooks. Store historical data in various formats, including images and e-forms, for comprehensive documentation.
  • Extraordinary Expenses Tracking: Effortlessly assess the fair value of damaged goods and track associated costs. Record past expenses to ensure accurate compensation for extraordinary losses.
  • Actionable Reminders: Never miss a crucial step during disaster recovery. Receive timely reminders to upload invoices and essential documents, all tailored to your location and the possibility of disaster conditions.
  • Disaster Resource Guidance: Stay informed about state and federal disaster relief program eligibility. Prisere provides in-app reminders and assistance for payment and electronic transfers during disasters.

High-Priority Features for Enhanced Resilience:

  • Real-time Disaster Notifications: Prisere will notify you when there's a disaster in your area, ensuring you're promptly informed and can take necessary action to safeguard your business.
  • Revenue Stream Categorization: Effortlessly categorize revenue streams (eCommerce vs. in-store) to gain deeper insights into your financial data during business interruption.
  • Bank Connection for Swift Payments: Facilitate immediate claims payment during disasters with the ability to receive bank wire or electronic deposit options directly through the app.

With Prisere, you can proactively navigate disruptions, recover faster, and minimize the impact of disasters on your business. Join the ranks of innovative SMBs taking charge of their resilience journey.
